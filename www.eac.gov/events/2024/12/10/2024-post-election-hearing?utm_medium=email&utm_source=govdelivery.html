2024 Post-Election Hearing

Start Time
Tuesday, December 10, 2024 9:00 AM

End Time
Tuesday, December 10, 2024 12:00 PM

On December 10, the U.S. Election Assistance Commission (EAC) held its 2024 Post-Election Hearing at the Los Angeles County Registrar-Recorder/County Clerk’s Ballot Processing Center in City of Industry, CA. This event was livestreamed on the EAC's YouTube channel. 

During the meeting, election officials joined the EAC’s Commissioners for in-depth panel discussions on the 2024 elections. Topics included election security, contingency planning, and serving voters.

WHEN: Tuesday, December 10; 9 a.m. to 12 p.m. PT

WHERE: Los Angeles County Registrar-Recorder/County Clerk, Ballot Processing Center, Training Room, 13401 Crossroads Parkway N, City of Industry, CA 91746

PUBLIC REGISTRATION LINK: Please only register if planning to attend in person. Registration is not required to watch the livestream.
