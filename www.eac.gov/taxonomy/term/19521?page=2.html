New Hampshire Voter Info

Tuesday, November 12, 2024

New Hampshire

Local Election Office Directory

State Election Office Website

State Election Office Phone Number:

603-271-3242

Voter Registration Information

Voting Information
Register to Vote Absentee/ Mail and/or Early Voting
Update Your Registration Track Your Ballot
Check Your Registration In-Person Voting Location Lookup
Cancel Your Registration: Contact your local election office.  Military Voters
  Overseas Citizen Voters
Update Political Party: This is the same process as registering or updating your registration. You may change your party affiliation with the clerk of the town or city where you are domiciled.   

 

Registration Deadline
13 days before Election Day by mail*

*Only voters who are eligible for absentee voting can register by mail.

*Before the election, the last day to register is the last meeting of the supervisors of the checklist. By law, the supervisors meet once, 6-13 days before each state election. Check your town/city website, or call your clerk's office for the date, time, and location of the supervisor's meeting.

*A person qualified to vote who is not registered, may register and vote on Election Day at the polling place for the town or city ward where the person is domiciled.

Updated on November 12, 2024.

Cancel Your Registration Military Voters

Update Political Party: This is the same process as registering or updating your registration.  

 

Registration Deadline
18 days before Election Day by mail and online*
11 days before Election Day in-person

*Mail-in voter registrations must be mailed and postmarked on or before the third Friday preceding an election.
