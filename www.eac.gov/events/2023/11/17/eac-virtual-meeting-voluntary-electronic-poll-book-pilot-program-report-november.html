EAC Virtual Meeting on the Voluntary Electronic Poll Book Pilot Program Report - November 17, 2023

Start Time
Friday, November 17, 2023 2:00 PM

End Time

The U.S. Election Assistance Commission (EAC) hosted a virtual public meeting on the 2023 EAC Voluntary Electronic Poll Book Pilot Program Report. The event reviewed report findings and discussed testing and certification programs for electronic poll books or e-poll books.

During the meeting, the EAC Commissioners moderated panels to discuss the pilot program, the certification and testing of e-poll books, and the impact of these programs on state and local jurisdictions. Panelists included pilot participants, e-poll book developers, Voting System Test Laboratories (VSTLs), and state and local election administrators. The Commissioners also heard a presentation from Jay Phelps, Director of the EAC’s Election Supporting Technology Evaluation Program (ESTEP), on the pilot report.

WHAT: The U.S. Election Assistance Commission (EAC) Public Meeting on the Voluntary Electronic Poll Book Pilot Program Report

WHEN: Friday, November 17, 2023

WHO: Speakers included the following:

  • TJ Burns, Sr. State Certification Manager, Election Systems & Software
  • Jack Cobb, Laboratory Director, Pro V&V Inc
  • Scott Jarrett, Director of Elections, Maricopa County, Arizona
  • Dr. Chad Kinsella, Associate Professor of Political Science and Co-Director of Voting System Technical Oversight Program (VSTOP) at Ball State University
  • Jay Phelps, Director of the EAC’s Election Supporting Technology Evaluation Program (ESTEP)
  • Michael Santos, Voting System Test Lab (VSTL) Director, SLI Compliance
  • Rokey Suleman, Government Affairs Director, KNOWiNK
  • Mark Wlaschin, Nevada Deputy Secretary of State

WHERE: Live streamed on the EAC’s YouTube Channel.
