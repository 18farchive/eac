Working Together: Supporting the 2024 Elections through Federal Partnerships

The U.S. Election Assistance Commission (EAC) hosted a discussion with representatives from key federal agencies to discuss the ways they are working together to support local and state election officials as they prepare for the 2024 elections.

2024 Elections Summit - US Election Assistance Commission and the University of Maryland

Election officials, key stakeholders, and the EAC Commissioners met on the University of Maryland campus for in-depth panel discussions on topics including Confidence In Elections and Election Security, Serving All Voters, Challenges For New Election Officials, and Communicating About Elections.

EAC Public Meeting: Voter Education and Engagement

The U.S. Election Assistance Commission (EAC) held a public discussion on best practices, research, and EAC resources for voter education and engagement.
