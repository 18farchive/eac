Clearinghouse Resources on Audits & Recounts

Wednesday, November 13, 2024
Image

The resources below are designed to help election officials conduct election verification processes like audits and recounts.


Please refer to our main Clearinghouse Resources page for more best practices, tools, and guidance on other topics. 

 

 

 

Not finding what you are looking for?

Visit Clearinghouse Resources on Results, Canvassing & Certification to find additional resources. 

Audits & Recounts Resources

  • Communicating About Audits

    Resource providing quick tips for communicating about election audits with the public, as well as an activity prompt election officials can use to practice.

    View the PDF

  • Post-Election Tabulation Audit Guide

    Guide providing an introduction to the process of conducting different kinds of post-election tabulation audits, as well as the statistics involved. 

    View the PDF

  • Post-Election Tabulation Audits: A Brief Overview

    Brief covering the basics of post-election tabulation audits, including defining an audit, discussing why we audit, and describing what is needed to audit.

    View the PDF

  • Sample Handout: Election Audits

    Editable 1-pager describing common election audits for a general audience, with placeholders that election officials can fill in to reflect their own processes. 

    Download the File

  • Election Audits Across the United States

    This document provides insight on the following topics related to audits: types of audits, timing, policies, case studies, and state-specific information.

    View the PDF

  • Post-election Tabulation Audits

    Research paper describing state policies and procedures for post-election tabulation audits across the United States circa 2020.

    View the PDF

  • Quick Start Guide: Post-election Audits

    Brief guide intended to familiarize local election officials with the basic steps to audit an election.

    View the PDF

  • Chapter 17: Post-election Audits

    Chapter 17 of the 2nd edition of the Election Management Guidelines, published in 2023, covers best practices and key considerations related to conducting post-election audits.

     

    View the PDF

  • Chapter 19: Recounts

    Chapter 19 of the 2nd edition of the Election Management Guidelines, published in 2023, covers best practices and key considerations related to conducting recounts.

     

    View the PDF

  • Quick Start Guide: Developing an Audit Trail - Election Day

    Brief resource covering basic information about best practices to keep track of election equipment and ballots before, during, and after election day.

    View the PDF

  • Quick Start Guide: Conducting a Recount

    Guide covering the basic best practices for recounting ballots after an election.

    View the PDF
