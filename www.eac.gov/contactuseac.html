Contact U.S. EAC

Friday, October 18, 2024

If you have questions about voter registration, voting in your state, or need contact information for your state or local office please visit www.eac.gov/vote. 

For your safety and security, please do not include any personally identifiable information (PII) when submitting a question or comment to the EAC. PII includes but is not limited to full or partial Social Security numbers, driver's license numbers, dates of birth, and mailing addresses.
